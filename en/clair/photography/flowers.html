Flowers

For these photos I use the big zoom lens just like for the animals and I put the priority aperture mode. To have the blur it’s excatly the same settings than animals with a large aperture (~4.5) and the same focus on the center of the flower. In contrary of the animals photos I don’t have to put the full manually mode because flowers are fixe so it’s not necassary to adjust the speed.
