Mood Video

This video is a project I had to do for the video editing class in Vanier College. For this project, we had to do a video that reflect a mood and that show multiple camera angles.

My video

How I made my video ?

Shooting

For this video the main difficulty was to shoot differents camera angles by myself and playing inside the video at the same time. To do that I just shoot inside ma bedroom to have mutiple places to put the camera. I also prepared the shoot by drawing storyboards of the different camera angles. Before doing the final video we had to send a kind of trailer with all the different shots and the name of each shots.

Editing

This video was a little bit easier to edit because I just put all the shots side by side. Of course it was harder than that, I had to carefull of the details, the good rythm of the video and the consistency of all the sequences to tell a understanding story.
