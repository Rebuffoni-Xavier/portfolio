Looping Videos

These videos are a project I had to do for the video editing class in Vanier College. It was also a new project for me and I learn a lot of things doing this project.

My videos

How I made my video ?

Shooting

To make these videos I just shoot a short video with a main elements and that's all because the looping effects is made in editing part of the project.

Base Videos

Editing

For this project, the editing part was harder because to do a looping just for a part of the video, we have to draw around the main subject with the colors option in Davinci Resolve. Then, we have to cut the part we have drawn and duplicate the base video. After that we have two videos, one without the main subject and the other without any change. To have the main subject moving and the rest fixed, we have to freeze the video without the main subject and put it hover the base video (vice-versa if you want to freeze the main subject and move the rest). Finally, to loop the video we just have to duplicate the previous editing, put the second one after the first one and put the second one in the other way.
