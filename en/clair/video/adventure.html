Adventure Video

Français

This video is a project I had to do for the video editing class in Vanier College at Montreal. For this project, we had to choose a type of moovie (adventure, horror, thrilller, etc...) and then made a video in this type of moovie. I have choosen adventure movie and I have made a video of a woman who walk in the nature.

My video

How I made my video ?

Storyboard

Before shooting my video, I made a storyboard to imagine my story and what kind of shots I want.

Shooting

This video was very interesting to shoot because it was like a real movie, I had to find an actress (a person I know) for my video and a set. The main difficulty was to think about everything to not miss a shot. The second difficulty was to shot outside with the cold. We shot the video in 3 hours and we were freezing. The last difficulty was to be like a real movie director who is giving some concrete actions or feelings to do for the actress.

Editing

The editing was also very interesting to do because it was like a real movie. I put all the shots side by side with the order I had imagined before in my storyboard. I also put a special effect to inscrease the contrast between all the colors inside the shots. Finally I have choosen a background music to have a good atmosphere.
