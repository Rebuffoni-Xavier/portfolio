How do I take photos of sunsets ?

The sunset photos settings are exactly the same than the landscape but the focus is more important to have the right luminosity. In fact if I adjust the focus on the bright part, the photo will be too dark and vice-versa if I adjust the focus on the dark part. So, to have the right luminosity, I adjust the focus on the main part I want to take and I set manually the luminosity.
