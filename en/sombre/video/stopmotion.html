Stop Motion Video

This video is a project I had to do for the video editing class in Vanier College. This project was new for me, I had not did a stop motion project before.

My video

How I made my video ?

Shooting

To shoot this video I made all the elements and main characters by myself using basic things like aluminium or paper. After that I made a kind of set and I put the camera on one specific place. Finally I take a lot of photos with little change each time to do the movement effect.

Some photos of the stop motion video

Editing

To edit my video, I used Davinci Resolve and more particulary the sequence option of Davinci Resolve. The principe is simple : I rename all my photos with the number corresponding to the place they will have on the video and I import all the pictures like a sequence of images. Davinci Resolve set the duration of each photos automaticaly to do a good stop motion video with fluide movement. Finally I adjust the duration of each sequences to have the same thing I imagined at the begining.
