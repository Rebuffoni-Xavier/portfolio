This video is a project I had to do for the video editing class in Vanier College. I choose to do a video on the CINEMANIA event in Montréal.

My video

How I made my video ?

Shooting

To shoot this video I took my camera every time I went to see a movie screening as part of the event. I took some background shots with the people in the theater or the exteriror view of the theater. For the interviews, I had prepared some questions about the event and I asked 3 peoples after one of the movie screening.

Editing

To edit my video, I used Davinci Resolve. To have a consistency within in the video I alterned between background shots and interviews. Moreover I tryed to adapt the background shots with the interviews.
