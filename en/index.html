About Me

Hi, my name is Xavier REBUFFONI, I am a photographer and video creator at Montbéliard. I am in second year of MMI (Métier du Multimédia et de l'Internet) in Montbeliard. I really love take photos and shooting videos on differents subjects. I have a lot of technical skills in photography and video with a learning in a school specialized in the web.

Photography

Landscapes

Sunsets

I never plan the photos I will take in advance. When I see something beautiful, I take a picture of it and see how it looks. Sometimes I do several tests with different camera angles before making the perfect photo. I really like to walk in different places and suddenly have an idea of a photo. I am particularly sensitive to landscapes, sunsets, flowers, city and animals which are themes that come up regularly in my photos. I take all my photos with a Canon EOS R10.
