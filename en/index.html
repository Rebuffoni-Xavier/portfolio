About Me

Hi, my name is Xavier REBUFFONI, I am in second year of MMI (Métier du Multimédia et de l'Internet). I'm passionate about photography and cinema. I really love take photos and see a lot of movies or TV Show. I have a lot of technical skills in photography and video with a learning in a school specialized in the web.

I never plan the photos I will take in advance. When I see something beautiful, I take a picture of it and see how it looks. Sometimes I do several tests with different camera angles before making the perfect photo. I really like to walk around different places and suddenly have an idea of a photo and then take it. I am particularly sensitive to landscapes, sunsets, flowers, city and animals which are themes that come up regularly in my photos. I take all my photos with a Canon EOS R10.
